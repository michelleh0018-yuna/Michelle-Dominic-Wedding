Our Story

✦
2018

First Met

We first crossed paths at a mutual friend's gallery opening in San Francisco. James was admiring the wrong painting, and Emma couldn't help but correct him — the rest, as they say, is history.

2020

First Trip Together

A spontaneous weekend in Big Sur turned into a week-long adventure. Somewhere between the redwoods and the sea, we both knew this was something extraordinary.

2022

Moving In

We found a little apartment in the Mission District with a crooked kitchen and a perfect view. We turned it into a home.

2024

The Proposal

On a crisp November evening at Point Reyes, James got down on one knee. Emma said yes before he even finished the question.
